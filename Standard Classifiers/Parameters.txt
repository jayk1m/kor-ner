Notes on Parameters
• kNN
1. The Euclidean distance has been used for metric purposes.
2. For nominal values, a distance of 0 has been taken if two neighbors share the same nominal value while a distance of 1 has been taken otherwise.

• Decision Tree
1. Information gain has been used as the criterion for splitting the tree.
2. Maximal depth of 5 has been used as a parameter.

• Random Forest
1. Total number of trees of 40 was taken.
2. Information gain has been used as the criterion for splitting a tree.
